Welcome to Mild-Mannered Bloodhound! I have dedicated years in the BloodHound Breed. Our dogs are family raised with our children and are highly socialized! All of our dogs are AKC registered along with American bloodlines. Our daily lives are dedicated towards the care of our dogs and puppies, and they are very much a part of our family. Our bloodhounds are responsibly bred to maintain the form and function of the breed. We follow the American BloodHound Club.

Our Objectives

Our goal is to produce healthy bloodhound puppies for sale with well rounded out going personalities. Our focus is improving the overall quality of our bloodhounds. We proudly follow the AKC Breed Standards and our parent dogs are health tested. We invest a great amount of time and research into developing a healthy and stable companion for your whole family. Our Spacious home allow us to train and socialize the puppies every day. Our family raises our puppies in our home around our children giving them the best start in life with great temperaments. Our certifications demonstrate our cleanliness and the integrity of our operations. Remember to always work with a certified breeder.
